What we do

Here at "Find my Opponents", our main goal is to help futsal teams to find opponents the time they want at the place they want.

We realize that many futsal field owners do not have the resources to find opponents for the requesting teams so we came up with the idea of this website, allowing them to post their interested teams on the site so you and your team can look at your options and decide who, where and when you want to play.

Enjoy the service and if you have any questions or suggestions please feel free to contact us at once!
